# Privacy Policy for Bytes

**Last Updated: February 9, 2026**

**Package Name:** com.bytetobite.store

## Introduction

Bytes ("we," "our," or "the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Camera and Photo Library Access

Bytes requests access to your device's **camera** and **photo library** for the following purposes:

- **Food Photo Capture:** The camera is used to take photos of food items so our AI can identify them and generate recipes.
- **Inventory Scanning:** The camera is used to scan food items and add them to your personal inventory.
- **Photo Library Selection:** You may choose existing photos or videos from your gallery to analyze for recipe generation.

**We do not:**

- Access your camera in the background
- Record or stream video without your knowledge
- Use the camera for any purpose unrelated to food and recipe identification
- Share your photos with third parties for advertising or tracking purposes

### Account Information

If you create an account, we collect:

- Email address
- Username
- Encrypted password or passkey credentials

### Usage Data

We may collect anonymized usage data to improve the App, including:

- Features used and frequency
- App performance metrics
- Crash reports

## How We Use Your Information

- **Recipe Generation:** Photos and videos you submit are processed by our AI services (Google Gemini, Perplexity) to identify food and generate recipes.
- **Inventory Management:** Scanned food items are stored in your account to provide personalized recipe suggestions.
- **Cooking History:** Records of recipes you've cooked are stored to track your cooking activity.
- **Shopping Lists:** Shopping list data is stored to provide persistence across sessions.

## Data Storage and Security

- Your account data is stored securely in our database
- Uploaded media files are processed and not permanently stored on our servers
- We use industry-standard encryption for data transmission (HTTPS/TLS)
- Authentication tokens are stored securely on your device

## Third-Party Services

The App integrates with the following third-party services:

- **Google Gemini AI:** For food recognition and recipe generation from photos and videos
- **Perplexity AI:** For enhanced recipe search and generation
- **RevenueCat:** For subscription and in-app purchase management
- **YouTube / Social Media Platforms:** For processing recipe video URLs you provide

These services have their own privacy policies, and we encourage you to review them.

## Subscriptions

Bytes offers optional premium subscriptions managed through RevenueCat. Payment processing is handled by Apple (App Store) and Google (Play Store). We do not directly collect or store your payment information.

## Children's Privacy

Bytes is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

## Your Rights

You have the right to:

- **Access** the personal data we hold about you
- **Delete** your account and associated data
- **Revoke** camera and photo library permissions at any time through your device settings
- **Opt out** of any optional data collection

## Data Retention

- Account data is retained until you delete your account
- Uploaded media is processed in real-time and not permanently stored
- Cooking history and saved recipes are retained until you delete them or your account

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy.
